# Product Roadmap

## Vision

WorkSphere AI will evolve into a complete AI-powered SaaS platform that manages the entire employee lifecycle.

The roadmap is divided into multiple releases to ensure incremental delivery while continuously improving the platform.

---

# Version 1.0 (MVP)

## Goal

Deliver a working platform that demonstrates the complete employee lifecycle.

### Features

### Authentication
- Register
- Login
- JWT Authentication
- Role-Based Access Control

### Company Management
- Company Profile
- Departments
- Teams

### Recruitment
- Job Openings
- Candidate Profiles
- Applications
- Offer Management

### Employee
- Employee Profiles
- Organization Structure
- Onboarding Checklist

### Learning
- Quiz Management
- Quiz Attempts
- Certificates

### Community
- Forum
- Comments
- Likes

### Recognition
- Employee Recognition
- Points System
- Employee of the Month

### Notifications
- In-App Notifications

### Dashboard
- Basic Analytics
